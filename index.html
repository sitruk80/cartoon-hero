Wolverine

About Wolverine

Wolverine is a character in the Marvel Universe. He was a member of the XMEN as well as many other groups over the years.

Wolverines Mutant Abilities

  1. He has a healing factor that cures any wound, and cures any disease. That healing factor also slows his aging, making him look younger than he is.
  2. He has bone claws. Later a government program known as Weapon X bonded his bones with adamantium giving him the metal claws that everyone knows and loves.
  3. He has heightned senses including the sense of smell that allows him to track a specific person.
